This pack was inspired by the twisted and TobLite packs to make a Tombs of Amascut centered pack. The pack was designed around the invocation minimalist style icons paired with the decadence of golden filigree on a somber back note of navy blues to evoke the design decisions behind the raid itself. Additionally the pack was filled with a lot of raid flavor to pay homage to specific portions of the raid and certain interactions with the UI and clicks are made to feel reactive where possible. This is all the while ensuring that basic pvm core is untouched so your day-to-day scaping doesn't feel impacted.
